# Background Music Folder

Place the background songs you want to play here.

## How to use:
1. Copy your audio files (typically `.mp3`) into this folder:
   `happy birthday/artifacts/birthday/public/music/`
2. Name them `song1.mp3`, `song2.mp3`, and `song3.mp3` to match the default configuration, or open `src/config.ts` and change the filenames in the `songs` list to match yours!
